How to Use Kindness Cup

Navigation Bar: using your computer mouse or trackpad, single click over the title of the page you wish to go to

Menu Items: To find an item on the menu, click on the searchbar and type in the item you wish to find. Once you have typed in the item, click the ~Search~ button. If your searched item is available it will show up underneath the search bar. If the item is unavailable or not in stock, a message will appear, letting you know. If you wish to view the menu by price, there is a selector near the top right of the page, underneath the navigation bar. click on this to see options of how to view the menu, and click on your chosen option for the menu to change to that view.

Adding items to your order: If you wish to add an item to your order, use the quantity type input bar on the item's box to either type in your desired quantity or click on the up and down arrows to find your desired quantity. Once you have selected your quantity, click on the button in the box, and this will add it to your order.

The Drink Order form: Click on the Customer Name text box and type in your name. Click on the Customer Phone number box and type in your phone number. Click on the coffee type selector and click on the drink you would like to order. Click on the milk type selector and choose which type of milk you would like. Please expect that some milk types cost extra. Click on the size selector and choose what size drink you would like. Click on the temperature selector and choose the desired temperature of your drink. Click the circle to the right of which option of caffeine you would like. Click on the textbox and type in a number of how many sugars you would like in your drink. Click on the circle to the right of the payment option you would like to use. Click on the ~Complete~ button once you are happy with your order, and it will be added to your order.

Finalising your order: Once on the final order page, make sure all of your items are correct. If they are, click the ~Order Now~ button and it will be sent to the cafe and prepared for you. When you arrive at the cafe, make sure to state the name you placed the order under so that the correct order is brought to you.
